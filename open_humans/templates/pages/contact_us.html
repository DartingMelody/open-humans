Want to make some of your data public?

Public data can be a great good for society. In many cases, the data you've connected to Open Humans wouldn't otherwise be publicly shared due to privacy concerns – but privacy preferences vary. To learn more, read about our Public Data Sharing study.

Public Data Sharing study

For questions and concerns about the Public Data Sharing study, you may contact:

  • Study staff at support@openhumans.org.
  • The Principal Investigator (Madeleine Ball, PhD) at madeleine@openhumans.org.

Questions about security?

We take security seriously. In addition to ensuring that we use secure methods for our website and data, we've developed a set of security guidelines for research partner websites to follow. Read them in full at https://www.openhumans.org/community-guidelines/#study-websites.

If you've discovered a security issue, please contact us privately at security@openhumans.org.

General questions

For general questions about the Open Humans site, write to us at support@openhumans.org.
